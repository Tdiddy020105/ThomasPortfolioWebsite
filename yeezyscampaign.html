Introduction

The goal of this project was to make Yeezys appealing to people aged 65 and older. These shoes are primarily popular among young people, but we aim to make them more accessible to the older generation.

Content

I conducted interviews with elderly individuals to understand their preferences when it comes to footwear, their purchasing habits, and whether they were familiar with the Yeezy brand. I discovered that comfort is a priority for older adults, but they also appreciate shoes that are visually appealing. Most of them purchase shoes through brochures and were unfamiliar with the Yeezy brand. Based on these insights, I created several brochure advertisements to appeal to this audience.
