Overview

This campaign promotes sustainability through creative public installations.

Details

The anti-littering campaign aims to encourage proper disposal of cigarette butts. It utilizes innovative designs like portable ashtrays and interactive bins that reward users for responsible behavior. These ideas were deployed in parks and city squares to make a visible difference.
