Introduction

In collaboration with my project group, we made the decision to establish our own hypothetical game studio named Studio 21. Our aim was to develop a tower defence game with a bakery theme, allowing players to actively participate in combat against enemies. This game is titled "Bread-Gone-Bad-TD."
